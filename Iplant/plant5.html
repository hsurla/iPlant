Fiddle Leaf

The Fiddle Leaf Fig features impressive, broad, and glossy leaves that are deeply lobed, resembling the shape of a fiddle or violin. These leaves can grow up to a foot or more in length, creating a bold and dramatic appearance. The plant has a tree-like growth habit with a single, thick stem, and it can reach significant heights under the right conditions. Fiddle Leaf Figs thrive in bright, indirect light, and they should be shielded from harsh, direct sunlight. Adequate watering is crucial – the soil should be kept consistently moist but not waterlogged, allowing the top few inches to dry out between waterings.

Monstera

The Monstera, scientifically known as Monstera deliciosa, is a distinctive and popular tropical houseplant known for its large, glossy, and uniquely shaped leaves. Commonly referred to as the "Swiss Cheese Plant" due to the characteristic holes and splits in its foliage, Monstera has become an iconic choice for indoor greenery. The leaves are typically heart-shaped when young and develop distinct fenestrations as they mature, creating a striking and ornamental appearance.

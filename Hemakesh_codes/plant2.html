Variegated Pothos

Variegated Pothos (Epipremnum aureum) is a beloved houseplant celebrated for its captivating foliage. The heart-shaped leaves are glossy and adorned with a striking marbled or speckled pattern of green and creamy-white hues, making it a visually appealing addition to any indoor space. This versatile plant has a trailing or climbing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets, shelves, or as an elegant cascading element in plant displays.
